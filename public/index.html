About Lila

Got a link? We will do the rest.

Lila is a fast, browser-first YouTube downloader that turns one URL into a clean MP4 or MP3 download.

How it works:

  • Paste the link
  • Pick MP4 or MP3
  • Download straight to your device

We stream directly to your browser, so the file goes to you - not into some server folder. Perfect for offline viewing, audio-only saves and keeping clips for research or editing.

Files & storage

We do not store your downloaded files on our servers. Downloads are delivered to your browser and saved on your device.

We keep things simple: no accounts, no download history, and no stored files on our servers.

Your downloads are handled by your browser and saved directly to your device.

What is Lila?

Lila is a fast, browser-first tool that turns a single link into an MP4 (video) or MP3 (audio) download.

How does it work?

Paste a link, preview, pick MP4 or MP3, and download. Your browser saves the file to your device.

Do you store my downloads or my links?

No, Lila does not store your files or keep a download history. Any history you see is on your own device/browser.

Do I need an account?

Nope, no sign-up needed.

Is it free?

Yes, Lila is free to use.

Which formats do you support?

MP4 (video) and MP3 (audio).

Can I choose quality?

Yes, choose your preferred quality in Settings before downloading.

Why does a download sometimes fail?

Common reasons:

  • The video is private, age-restricted, region-locked, or removed
  • The platform changed something
  • A VPN/adblock/network rule is interfering (try disabling VPN or a different link and retry)

Is it okay to use?

Please use Lila responsibly: only download content you have the rights to use, and credit creators when appropriate. If you are unsure about permissions, do not download it.

You are responsible for using Lila legally. Personal use is okay, commercial without permission is not.
